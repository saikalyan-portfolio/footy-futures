Game Rules

Predict Groups

  • Enter every group-stage score.
  • Tables update automatically using points, goal difference, and goals scored.
  • The top two from each group plus the eight best third-placed teams qualify.

Build Knockouts

  • Round of 32 slots auto-populate from your group predictions.
  • From R32 onward, enter 90-minute scores.
  • If tied, enter after-extra-time. If still tied, enter penalties.
